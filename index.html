Welcome to the VIT Book Lending Platform!

Your go-to place for borrowing and sharing books.

About the Project

Our platform aims to foster a sense of community among students by allowing them to lend and borrow books easily. Whether you need a textbook for a semester or a novel for leisure reading, we’ve got you covered!

Features

  • Easy book lending and borrowing process.
  • Connect with fellow students and expand your reading list.
  • Track your borrowed books and return dates.
  • Rate and review books to help others make informed choices.

Stay Updated!

We are actively developing this platform and will notify you once it’s live. Follow us for the latest updates and features.
